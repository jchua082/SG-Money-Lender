About Us

Find out more about our founding story.

What We Do

Our company is a professional moneylending / credit company established in 2019 founded by Amy Lee. We are a reliable organisation registered with the Ministry of Law with high customer satisfaction ratings. Whether you need a large sum of money for school fees or smaller sums for emergencies arising, SG Money Lender is your go-to lender in Singapore.

Our Story

How it all began

It all started in March 2019 when our founder, Amy Lee’s father was diagnosed with a stage 4 colon cancer. Amy had just lost her job and was facing financial difficulties paying off the medical bills. She was also having trouble getting a bank loan due to her unemployment status. Fortunately, she was able to get help from her family and friends to overcome her financial hardships. Her father has also successfully battled cancer and is living healthily today.


The Start of SG Money Lender

However, not everybody could be as lucky as Amy. Determined to help others through financial hardships, Amy along with Derrick Lee decided to establish SG Money Lender in March 2020. Through her own experience with financial difficulties, she is able to empathise with our customer better and lead our team, providing our customers with the support they need.


How have we grown?

Today, we have expanded our business to 4 branches island-wide in Singapore with 20 employees ready to serve your needs. In the past 2 years, we have also served more than 5000 customers from all walks of lives, helping them overcome obstacles and achieving important milestones by eliminating the monetary hurdle. We will strive to help as many people to achieve a better quality of life without financial worries.
