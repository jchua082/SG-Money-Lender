Loan Types

We offer lenient loan tenure and low interest rates to as low as 2.27%.

Personal Loans

Instant approval and cash disbursement of up to S$50,000 with a low interest rate of 3.4% p.a.. Applicants must be at least 21 years old with a minimum annual income of S$30,000. For Singaporeans and PRs only.

Business Loans

This loan can be used for your business expansion projects, cash flow issues, asset acquisition, etc. Loan amount can go up to S$500,000 with a low interest rate of 2.6% p.a..

Foreigner Loans

Loan up to S$12,000 with a minimum repayment amount of $200 a month. Only foreigners with an employment pass of 1-year validity and a minimum annual income of S$40,000 are eligible.

Education Loans

Loan up to 90% of your subsidized tuition fees with 0% interest rate during your course of study. Only applicable for Singaporean students in tertiary education.

Medical Loans

This loan covers all your medical costs that is not covered by your insurance or Medishield. e.g. Hospitalisation Fees, Medical Tests, Follow-up Visits

Wedding Loans

This loan covers your wedding expenses from your engagement rings to your honeymoon. Borrow up to 6 times your monthly income with no upfront fees.

Vacation Loans

This loan can be used to cover all vacation expenses. Take loans up to 6 times your monthly salary with interest rates from as low as 2.27%. No property or assets collateral required.

Renovation Packages

Loan up to 6 times your monthly salary for all renovation expenses. Enjoy attractive interest rate from 3.2% p.a. Loan tenure up to 5 years.
